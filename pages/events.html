Sapporo Snow Festival

Experience the magic of winter at one of the world's largest snow festivals held in Sapporo's Odori Park. Admire magnificent snow sculptures, enjoy delicious local cuisine, and immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere.

Sapporo Snow Festival Official Website

Snow Miku Festival

Dive into the world of Hatsune Miku's winter persona, Snow Miku, at this festival in Sapporo. Enjoy live concerts, art exhibitions, and themed merchandise celebrating this beloved virtual character.

Snow Miku Festival Official Website

Furano Lavender Fields Festival

Wander through fields of blooming lavender in Furano during the summer Lavender Fields Festival. Experience the breathtaking beauty of these purple landscapes and explore local attractions and activities.

Furano Lavender Fields Festival Official Website

Asahikawa Winter Festival

Venture to Asahikawa for a winter wonderland experience at the Asahikawa Winter Festival. Marvel at stunning snow sculptures, enjoy ice-related activities, and witness dazzling light displays.

Asahikawa Winter Festival Official Website

Otaru Fog Festival

Step into a magical world of artificial fog and gas lamps at the Otaru Fog Festival held during the summer. Explore the atmospheric streets of Otaru and discover its charm amidst the mist.

Otaru Fog Festival Official Website

Hellfire Symphony

Experience the breathtaking fusion of nature and pyrotechnics as the Valley of Hell in Hokkaido comes alive with a spectacular display of demon-themed fireworks. Against the rugged backdrop of volcanic landscapes, this fiery spectacle paints the night sky with an enchanting symphony of light and color, offering a truly unforgettable experience for all who witness it.
